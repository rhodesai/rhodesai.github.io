About RAIL

The Rhodes Artificial Intelligence Lab (RAIL) is an action lab and consultancy that uses AI to solve real problems.

Our interdisciplinary teams work with senior scholars and friends in 2-month, structured engagements to solve real-life data challenges they and their organization are facing.

RAIL also hosts AI-related events around Rhodes House.

Joining RAIL

RAIL is a technical and non-technical team of current Rhodes Scholars. Our technical team is composed of AI and machine learning engineers, statisticians, and computer scientists. Our non-technical team is composed of ex-consultants, domain specialists in areas like health/education/energy, and others.

We're always looking for interesting people from all backgrounds to be involved in RAIL. If you know or want to know more about AI, want to solve problems either through code or through thinking, and want to make an impact, please email us at rhodesailab [at] gmail.com
